About me

“The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step”. As a travel fanatic, and for the last 11 years, I have been assisting people in different ways, guiding their next steps. Travels not only brings open-mindedness, empathy and tolerance, it also refines curiosity and creativity, all the main ingredients for a fulfilled life. Past experiences have led me to organize full tours worldwide, accompany and entertain my fellow travelers through their project and deal with a variety of critical situations, always using knowledge, positive energy an humor as navigation rules. Either through free guided tours in Brussels, couchsurfing, yearly radio shows or work, sharing and guiding people through their travels has been more than an everyday activity, it simply is who i am.
